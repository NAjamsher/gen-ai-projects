How routing works:
Step 1 — Intent chain reads your message and classifies it into one of 5 intents.
Step 2 — Router function checks the intent and sends your message to the matching chain.
Step 3 — That chain processes your message and returns the result.
One input. Five possible paths. The AI decides which one.
